What's Boostr?
Boostr connects highschool STEM teams with potential sponsors. It's a way for engineering and science people to easily fundraise, and tell their stories.

Everyone here at Boostr is on either our school's robotics team, science olympiad team, a VEX team, or science club. We're passionate about building robots, competing, and doing science, and fundraising is an integral part of making that feasible.

We'll connect you with your community, so that you can do what you love, instead of fumbling for funding.
